Conclusion

  • Bias is defined as a pattern of association between a particular decision and a particular sex of applicant, of sufficient strength to make us confident that it is unlikely to be the result of chance alone

  • Discrimination is defined as the exercise of decision influenced by the sex of the applicant when that is immaterial to the qualifications for entry

  • Bias is not the same as Discrimination
